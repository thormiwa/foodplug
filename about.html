ABOUT

Okafor Nneka Tomiwa, a Nigerian, is the writer, recipe developer and photographer behind the award winning site, The food PLUG. Tomiwa has been sharing her easy, innovative and healthy recipes with her readers since August 2020. More recently, she started diving into the tech world .

Tomiwa is a Nigerian at heart, growing up in the beautiful cosmopolitan city of Lagos, She is regularly inspired by Nigerian flavors, drawing off of her mum’s youth in Ondo and her parents’ time in Enugu during the early part of their marriage.

If you are interested in working with Tomiwa, or would like to request a copy of her media page, please email her at thormiwa04@gmail.com.

Tomiwa aspires to give you recipes that fit into your lifestyle during hectic weekdays and leisurely weekend get-togethers. Take a look around in the Recipe Index to find plenty of inspiration for your next meal.
